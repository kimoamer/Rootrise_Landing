What Drives Us

Our mission to transform SME growth through innovation.

DEVONEERS is a purpose-driven organization committed to enabling inclusive economic growth by tackling the persistent barriers that constrain SME transformation in fragile and transitioning economies.

SME transformation today is costly, fragmented, and slow. Enterprises spend heavily on training and consulting, yet remain trapped in outdated systems, unstructured processes, and weak management practices that prevent them from scaling or attracting investment. Traditional interventions take months to produce static reports and rarely leave behind continuity or measurable progress.

"Digitization and AI adoption remain surface-level, limited to generic tools rather than embedded in core workflows and decision-making."

This gap is a missed opportunity: SMEs form the backbone of economies, yet lack access to affordable, effective, tech-enabled solutions. By reframing SME upgrading as a data- and AI-powered process, transformation can become faster, measurable, and affordable at scale, creating a blueprint for replication worldwide.
